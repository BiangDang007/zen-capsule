## Full Description (4000 chars max)

Zen Capsule creates a digital barrier around your focus time. When you start a session, all notifications are silently intercepted — only truly urgent messages break through.

**How it works:**
- Start a focus session (25, 45, 60, or 90 minutes — or set your own)
- All incoming notifications are blocked and analyzed by AI
- Urgent messages (emergencies, critical work alerts) break through instantly
- Everything else is saved for a summary when your session ends

**Smart AI Urgency Detection**
Powered by Claude AI, Zen Capsule understands context — not just keywords. It knows the difference between "the server is down" and "check out this new restaurant downtown." Your boss's urgent message gets through; promotional spam doesn't.

**Break Report**
After each session, review what was blocked in a clean summary organized by urgency level: Critical, Important, Normal, and Social. See exactly what you missed and provide feedback to improve AI accuracy over time.

**Whitelist & App Rules**
- Add VIP contacts (boss, family) who always break through
- Set per-app rules: always block shopping apps, always allow work chat
- Fine-tune what the AI analyzes vs. what gets auto-handled

**Privacy First**
Your notification content is analyzed server-side and never stored permanently. We don't sell your data. Period.

**Features:**
- Focus timer with preset and custom durations
- AI-powered notification urgency analysis
- Breakthrough notifications for truly urgent messages
- Session history with detailed break reports
- Whitelist for VIP contacts
- Per-app blocking/allowing rules
- Multi-language support (English, 繁體中文, العربية)
- Beautiful cream & orange minimal design

Take back your attention. Build your digital barrier with Zen Capsule.
