隱私權政策

PRIVACY POLICY · 最後更新 LAST UPDATED：2026-06-12

Zen Capsule 的核心功能是在你專注時攔截並分析通知。這意味著我們會經手你的通知內容——所以這份文件寫得格外仔細。我們不投放廣告、不使用追蹤 SDK、不販售你的資料。

1. 我們蒐集哪些資料

  • 帳號資料：註冊時的 email 與密碼（密碼以 bcrypt 雜湊保存，永不儲存明文）。
  • 通知內容：專注場次進行期間，被攔截通知的標題與內容摘要會以加密連線傳送至我們的伺服器，進行緊急程度分析。
  • 使用資料：專注場次的起訖時間、時長、攔截次數。
  • 偏好設定：白名單聯絡人、App 規則、語言設定。

2. 我們如何使用這些資料

  • 緊急程度分析：通知內容由 AI（Anthropic 的 Claude）分析，判斷訊息是否緊急到需要穿透你的專注場次。
  • 休息報告：產生回顧清單，讓你在休息時查看專注期間被攔下的訊息。
  • 服務改善：以去識別化、彙總後的使用統計改善 App。

3. 資料保存與刪除

  • 保存內容與期限：用於 AI 分析的通知內容會以行為紀錄（behavior log）形式與你的專注場次一併保存在你的帳號下，用於產生休息報告、供你回顧歷史，以及依你的回饋改善判斷準確度。這些紀錄會保存至你刪除它們或刪除帳號為止。
  • 帳號資料儲存於 Railway 託管的 PostgreSQL 資料庫。
  • 刪除帳號時，所有相關資料（含全部通知紀錄）將立即且永久刪除。刪除功能位於 App 的設定頁面，無需透過客服。

4. 第三方服務

  • Anthropic（Claude AI）：通知內容會傳送至 Anthropic API 進行緊急程度分析，適用 Anthropic 的隱私權政策。Anthropic 不會使用 API 輸入內容訓練模型。
  • Railway：後端基礎設施託管服務。
  • Google Play / RevenueCat：PRO 訂閱由 Google Play 處理付款，我們不會接觸你的信用卡資訊；訂閱狀態經由 RevenueCat 同步。

我們不使用任何廣告 SDK、分析追蹤器，也不會將你的資料販售給第三方。

5. App 權限

  • 通知存取（Notification Access）：攔截與分析通知是本 App 的核心功能，此權限為功能必要。僅在專注場次進行期間使用，你可隨時於系統設定撤銷。
  • 網路：與後端通訊（AI 分析、帳號管理）。
  • 震動：緊急訊息穿透時提醒你。

6. 資料安全

  • 所有傳輸一律 HTTPS 加密
  • 密碼以 bcrypt 雜湊
  • JWT 認證，access token 有效期僅 15 分鐘
  • 全部 API 端點皆有速率限制與登入鎖定保護
  • 輸入驗證與淨化，防止注入攻擊

7. 你的權利

  • 查閱：在 App 內查看你的場次歷史與休息報告。
  • 刪除：在設定頁面永久刪除帳號與所有資料。
  • 控制：透過白名單與 App 規則控制哪些通知會被分析。

8. 兒童隱私

Zen Capsule 不以未滿 13 歲兒童為對象，我們不會有意蒐集未滿 13 歲兒童的資料。

9. 政策變更

我們可能不時更新本政策；重大變更將透過 App 內通知或 email 告知。

10. 聯絡我們

對本政策有任何疑問，請來信：zencapsule.app@gmail.com

ENGLISH VERSION

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information: Email address and password (hashed with bcrypt, never stored in plain text).
  • Notification content: During active focus sessions, intercepted notification titles and preview text are sent over an encrypted connection to our server for AI urgency analysis.
  • Usage data: Focus session start/end times, duration, and the number of intercepted notifications.
  • User preferences: Whitelist contacts, app rules, and language settings.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Urgency analysis: Notification content is analyzed by AI (Claude by Anthropic) to determine if a message is urgent enough to break through your focus session.
  • Session reports: We generate break reports so you can review what was blocked during your focus time.
  • Service improvement: Anonymized, aggregated usage statistics help us improve the app.

3. Data Storage and Retention

  • What is kept and for how long: Notification content used for AI analysis is stored as behavior logs linked to your focus sessions, to generate break reports, let you review your history, and improve accuracy based on your feedback. These logs are retained until you delete them or delete your account.
  • Account data is stored in a PostgreSQL database hosted on Railway.
  • When you delete your account, all associated data — including all notification logs — is permanently removed immediately. Deletion is available in the app's Settings screen.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Anthropic (Claude AI): Notification content is sent to Anthropic's API for urgency analysis. Anthropic's privacy policy applies. Anthropic does not use API inputs to train models.
  • Railway: Backend infrastructure hosting.
  • Google Play / RevenueCat: PRO subscriptions are billed by Google Play — we never see your payment details; subscription status is synced via RevenueCat.

We do not use advertising SDKs or analytics trackers, and we do not sell your data.

5. Permissions

  • Notification Access: Required to intercept and analyze incoming notifications during focus sessions — the core functionality of the app. Used only while a focus session is active; revocable anytime in system settings.
  • Internet: Required to communicate with our backend.
  • Vibrate: Used to alert you when an urgent notification breaks through.

6. Data Security

  • HTTPS encryption for all data in transit
  • bcrypt password hashing
  • JWT-based authentication with short-lived (15-minute) access tokens
  • Rate limiting and login lockout on all API endpoints
  • Input validation and sanitization to prevent injection attacks

7. Your Rights

  • Access: View your session history and break reports in the app.
  • Deletion: Permanently delete your account and all associated data from the Settings screen.
  • Control: Configure whitelist contacts and app rules to control what is analyzed.

8. Children's Privacy

Zen Capsule is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes through the app or via email.
